Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping process

How long does vehicle shipping typically take? +

Shipping times vary based on distance, route, and weather conditions. Domestic shipments typically take 1-2 weeks, while international shipments may take 4-8 weeks including customs clearance.

What documents do I need for vehicle shipping? +

You'll typically need the vehicle title, registration, proof of insurance, and a valid ID. For international shipments, additional customs documentation may be required.

How does the tracking system work? +

Once your vehicle is in transit, you'll receive a unique tracking code. Use this code in our client portal to view real-time location updates and estimated delivery times.

What insurance coverage is provided? +

We provide comprehensive insurance coverage for your vehicle during transit. Coverage details and limits will be outlined in your shipping agreement.

How are shipping costs calculated? +

Shipping costs are based on vehicle type, distance, transport method, and current market conditions. Contact us for a personalized quote.
